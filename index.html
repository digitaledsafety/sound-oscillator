How to Play

  • Short Tap: Starts a pulsing preview sound. Tap again to layer sounds.
  • Long Press: Toggles a continuous note that follows your movement.
  • Double Tap: Stops and clears all currently playing sounds.
  • Tilt: Change the pitch by tilting your device front-to-back.
